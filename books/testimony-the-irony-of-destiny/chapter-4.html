Chapter Four — The Empty Side of Love

Three months had passed since Olabode traveled to Canada. At first, the distance didn’t feel real — he called every night, sent pictures.

But as time went on, his voice became like a radio with weak signal — still there, but fading.

Then one evening, Victoria showed Chioma a post: “Congratulations to Mr. & Mrs. Olabode Adeshina – Just Married, Toronto, Canada.”
