Chapter Two — The Weekend of Temptation

Friday nights at Osun State University had a heartbeat of their own. It began from the buzzing generator sounds to the laughter of students getting ready to “enjoy life.”

Inside Room 12 of the female hostel, Abiola and Victoria were painting their faces into confidence.

Chioma sat quietly at the edge of her bed, reading a book titled The Woman of Purpose.

Then her phone vibrated. It was a message from Olabode: “Hey, Angel, hope your day was fine?”
